About

FIND YOUR HIROSHIMA is on-line service for travelers who seek unique journey just for you.
Via direct communication with Hiroshima's local contents,
you will discover amazing destinations which fulfill your various interest.

HowtoUse

1. DISCOVERY MAP

Use DISCOVERY MAP and find places / people/ activities which attract your interest. The contents shown on the map will be updated regularly.

2. SMS/Video chat

You can directly communicate the people via SMS and Video chat function. Ask any questions. Automatic translation is available on SMS.

3. Visit & Meet up

Online direct communication will enable your smooth access to the destination. Meet up with your only one Hiroshima journey.
